# How it works
### Near the bottom of the screen you will be prompted with the word of what you must draw.

<img width="350" alt="Screen Shot 2024-05-02 at 12 08 50 PM" src="https://github.com/AnthonyValle/Pictionary-AI/assets/66498197/5ffabaa8-0d3c-4fd2-8027-baf1866ca028">

### For a prediction to appear you have the options to auto-guess or manually guess.
### If auto-guess is on the model will guess your drawing at the end of each drawing stroke.
### To manually guess you click Guess.
### You can also clear your canvas by clicking Clear.

<img width="350" alt="Screen Shot 2024-05-02 at 12 21 45 PM" src="https://github.com/AnthonyValle/Pictionary-AI/assets/66498197/1c804d9d-552f-4f71-bb9f-f5044ba25642">

### At the bottom of the screen there are two other options: Change Choice and Add Image To Model
### Change Choice changes the word of the image you must draw
### Add Image To Model adds the current image you just drew to the image file of the word you had to draw. This is essentially a pipeline to update the model's data making it more accurate
